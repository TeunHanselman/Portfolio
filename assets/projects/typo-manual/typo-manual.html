Typographic Manual

2024

17x24 cm

A collective publication project in which students were asked each to research, write, and design a chapter on a specific typographic concept/aspect. The resulting combinations of chapter would provide a comprehensive overview of typography. My chapter focuses on exploring the concept of Type Color, outlining what it means, its effects and how to work with it. Additionally, all participants designed their own front & back cover to their personal copy of the publication.
