Rewire Reflections

2025

Publication
10.5x28 cm
(29.7x42 cm when opened fully)

A publication project by Rewire Festival in collaboration with KABK. A group of students was asked to work on the second iteration of the Rewire Reflections, a project in which writers are invited to write about performances, interview artists and reflection on the themes of the festival, culminating in the creation of a zine.

We, as the designers, were in charge of the visual direction for this year’s zine. The general concept and visual framework was developed before the festival weekend, but most of the writing, and therefore final designing of the publication was all done during the festival itself. During the weekend we shared a space with all of the writers, collaborating with eachother and making sure everyone was satisfied. For the last day of the festival , 200 zines were riso printed overnight to host a folding party on the last day with all participants, after which the publications were handed out to visitors of the festival.

Accompanying this launch event, a panel was hosted discussing the current state and future of music journalism, during which the zine was featured.

Collaboration with Dasha Glushkova, Alexandria Vitko, Puck Pruim, Nina Meljac & Pyotr Golub
