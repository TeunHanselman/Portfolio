Metamorphosis Identity

2026

Poster
59.4x84.1 cm

IdenTity design for the KABK GD year 3 "Metamorphosis" exhibition. The identity features a series of poster each having undergone their own unique transformation communicating the concept of "Metamorphosis" in a physical tangible form. The posters themselves feature only a description of the process they underwent, and a general stamp with the exhibition information
