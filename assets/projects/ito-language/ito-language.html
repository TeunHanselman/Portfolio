ITO Language System

2025

Poster
59.4x84.1 cm

A poster documenting the development of a language system as a continuation of the InTermsOf project. The language system aimed to provide a modular glyph set for discussing and exploring the relation between sound and the visual. The system derives its shape from the circular motifs of music in physical form. Part of the development of this language system is documented in the ITO process book.
